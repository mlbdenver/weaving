Instructions and Help

To use the calculator:

  • 1. Enter warp inputs in Warp section
  • 2. Press "Calculate warp" to finish warp measurements
  • 3. Enter empty weft inputs
  • 4. Press "Calculate weft" to finish weft measurements

About

This weaving calculator and project planner was inspired by and based on a printed version created by Pamela Bliss, a well-known and loved weaving instructor in Denver. I hope you use it and enjoy it! Please send any feedback, feature requests, or questions to me using the contact form.
